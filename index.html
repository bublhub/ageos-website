01

Run Dozens of Local Agents and Models

Your hardware shouldn't limit how many AI agents you run. BubbleHub schedules workloads smartly across your machines and comes with specialized agents ready to deploy, with no custom build required.

02

Security Built In, Not Bolted On

Every agent is automatically sandboxed before it starts. A compromised or runaway agent stays contained, so your data and the rest of your system remain protected.

03

Access Only With Your Approval

When an agent needs to reach one of your apps, you get a prompt to approve or deny. Nothing connects without your explicit consent, so you stay in control every time.
